On our 3rd Anniversary...

My dearest love,

Three years ago, something magical happened — you walked into my life and everything changed. The world became brighter, warmer, and full of meaning I never knew before.

Every day with you is a gift. Your smile is my sunrise. Your laughter is my favorite sound. The way you care, the way you love — it makes me fall for you all over again, every single day.

Through every season, every storm and sunshine, you've been my home. You've loved me in ways I didn't know I needed. You've shown me what it means to truly belong somewhere.

Thank you for choosing me. Today, tomorrow, and always — I am yours completely.

Agar hum dono ko is rishte ko budhape tak le jaana hai, toh koi block, unblock, ya ignore nahi hogi. Na meri taraf se, na teri taraf se.

Baat karni hai toh baat karo. Side mat lo. Yahi humara waada hai — har cheez face karte hain, saath milke. ❤️
